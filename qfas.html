Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our institution

General Information

What are the admission requirements for Uniglobe SS/College?

Admission requirements include completed application forms, academic transcripts from previous institutions, entrance examination results, and a personal interview. Students must meet minimum academic standards and demonstrate commitment to learning. Please contact our admissions office for specific details and application deadlines.

What programs do you offer at Uniglobe SS/College?

We offer comprehensive programs including Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics), Management (Business Administration, Economics, Accounting), and Law (Constitutional Law, Criminal Law, Civil Law). Each program is designed to meet industry standards and prepare students for higher education and career success.

What is the student-teacher ratio at your institution?

We maintain an optimal student-teacher ratio of approximately 20:1 to ensure personalized attention and quality education. Our small class sizes allow for interactive learning, individual mentoring, and better academic support for each student.

What facilities are available on campus?

Our campus features modern classrooms with smart boards, well-equipped science laboratories, computer labs with latest software, a comprehensive library, sports facilities including a multi-purpose ground, cafeteria, health services, and Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the campus. We also have dedicated study areas and counseling services.

How can I access the MIS portal?

You can access the MIS portal by clicking the "MIS Portal" button in the navigation menu. Different user types (Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent) have separate login credentials. Contact the administration for your login credentials if you haven't received them.

What is the academic calendar and schedule?

Our academic year typically runs from April to March, with regular breaks for holidays and examinations. Classes are held Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, with additional activities and clubs available after school hours. Detailed academic calendars are provided to students at the beginning of each term.

Do you provide transportation services?

Yes, we provide safe and reliable transportation services covering major areas of Kathmandu. Our fleet of buses operates on scheduled routes with trained drivers and conductors. Transportation fees are included in the annual fee structure, and routes are designed to ensure convenient access for students.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports (football, basketball, cricket, athletics), cultural activities (music, dance, drama), academic clubs (science, debate, literature), community service programs, and leadership development initiatives. Students are encouraged to participate in activities that align with their interests and talents.

How do you support students with special needs?

We are committed to inclusive education and provide support for students with special needs through specialized teaching methods, accessible facilities, counseling services, and individualized learning plans. Our staff includes trained professionals who work closely with students and parents to ensure academic success and personal development.

What is the fee structure and payment schedule?

Our fee structure is transparent and includes tuition, laboratory fees, library access, and basic facilities. Fees can be paid annually, semi-annually, or monthly as per convenience. We also offer scholarships and financial aid for deserving students. Detailed fee breakdowns are provided during admission and are available in the administration office.

How do you ensure student safety and security?

Student safety is our top priority. We have comprehensive security measures including CCTV surveillance, trained security personnel, controlled access to campus, emergency response protocols, and regular safety drills. We also maintain strict visitor policies and ensure all staff undergo background checks.

What career guidance and counseling services do you provide?

We provide comprehensive career guidance through individual counseling sessions, career assessment tests, workshops on various career options, university application guidance, and regular interactions with industry professionals. Our counseling team helps students make informed decisions about their academic and career paths.

How do you communicate with parents about student progress?

We maintain regular communication with parents through parent-teacher conferences, progress reports, digital communication platforms, and the MIS portal where parents can access their child's academic records, attendance, and performance updates. We also have an open-door policy for parent consultations.

What is your approach to technology integration in education?

We integrate technology throughout our curriculum using smart classrooms, computer-aided learning, online resources, and digital assessment tools. Students learn essential digital skills while maintaining a balance with traditional learning methods. Our computer labs are equipped with the latest software and hardware.

How do you handle student discipline and behavior management?

We follow a positive discipline approach that focuses on character development, self-discipline, and mutual respect. Our behavior management system includes clear expectations, consistent consequences, counseling support, and opportunities for students to learn from mistakes and grow personally.

What is the school's policy on attendance and punctuality?

We emphasize regular attendance and punctuality as essential life skills. Students are expected to maintain at least 85% attendance. Absences must be communicated in advance, and medical certificates are required for extended absences. Regular attendance is linked to academic performance and participation in examinations.

How do you prepare students for higher education and careers?

We prepare students through rigorous academic programs, skill development workshops, career exploration activities, university application guidance, and industry exposure programs. Our alumni network and partnerships with universities and companies help students make informed decisions about their future education and career paths.

What measures do you take to maintain academic excellence?

We maintain academic excellence through qualified faculty, updated curriculum, regular assessments, continuous professional development for teachers, modern teaching methodologies, and a supportive learning environment. We also conduct regular academic reviews and implement improvements based on student performance and feedback.
