Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our institution

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements include completed application forms, academic transcripts, and entrance examination results. Please contact our admissions office for specific details.

What programs do you offer?

We offer a wide range of programs including Science, Arts, Commerce, and specialized courses in technology and management. Each program is designed to meet industry standards.

How can I access the MIS portal?

You can access the MIS portal by clicking the "MIS Portal" button in the navigation menu. Different user types (Admin, Teacher, Student, Parent) have separate login credentials.

What facilities are available on campus?

Our campus features modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a library, sports facilities, computer labs, and a cafeteria. We also provide Wi-Fi connectivity throughout the campus.
