About Uniglobe SS/College

Empowering minds, shaping futures, and bringing the best together

Our Institution

Uniglobe Secondary School, located in the heart of Kathmandu, is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and fostering academic excellence. Founded with a vision to create global leaders, we have been at the forefront of educational innovation for over a decade.

Our institution stands as a beacon of educational excellence, combining traditional values with modern teaching methodologies. We believe in nurturing not just academic excellence, but also character development, leadership skills, and social responsibility.

Diverse Programs

We offer comprehensive educational programs designed to meet the diverse needs and aspirations of our students:

Science Program

Our Science program provides a strong foundation in Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics. Students engage in hands-on laboratory work, research projects, and practical applications of scientific principles. The program prepares students for careers in medicine, engineering, research, and technology.

Management Program

The Management program focuses on business administration, economics, accounting, and entrepreneurship. Students develop practical business skills through case studies and real-world projects. This program prepares future business leaders, entrepreneurs, and corporate professionals.

Law Program

Our Law program introduces students to legal principles, constitutional law, and justice systems. Students participate in mock trials, legal research, and community service projects. The program prepares students for careers in law, judiciary, and legal advocacy.

Humanities Program

The Humanities program offers a comprehensive study of languages, literature, history, and philosophy. Students develop critical thinking, analytical skills, and a deep understanding of human culture and society. This program prepares students for careers in education, journalism, and cultural studies.

Hotel Management Program

The Hotel Management program provides comprehensive training in hospitality, tourism, and service management. Students learn about hotel operations, customer service, event management, and tourism industry practices. This program prepares students for careers in hospitality, tourism, and service industries.
